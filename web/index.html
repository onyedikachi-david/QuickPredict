Features

Everything you need to trade,
in one chat.

Non-custodial wallets

Generate a Sui keypair with /wallet create. Encrypted with AES-256-GCM via PBKDF2 (310k iterations). The server never holds your funds.

Sub-5s on-chain trades

Pre-built transaction blocks and gas-sponsored onboarding. Tap Confirm and your position lands on Sui in seconds.

Options-accurate pricing

Every preview shows premium, max payout, net profit, and implied probability — parsed from live SVI parameters.

Settlement keeper

A 20-second keeper checks expired positions, waits for settled oracles, and calls redeem_permissionless for winning binaries. DM on win.

Copy trading

Mirror another trader's next position with /copy @user. Each copy tracked independently in your own PnL.

Group tournaments

Timed competitions in any group with /tournament start. Live scoring, winner announced at expiry.

Process

Zero to live position.
Three commands.

1

Create your wallet

/wallet create mypassword

A new Sui keypair is generated, encrypted with your password, and stored locally. You get a 0x… address and onboarding testnet credits — 0.1 SUI for gas and 1,000 dUSDC to trade.

2

Pick a market

/markets

Browse every active oracle — BTC, ETH, SOL — whatever the registry returns. Live price, expiry countdown, and one-tap /up / /down shortcuts.

3

Place the trade

/up BTC 71000 10 100

Review the premium, tap Confirm, type your password once (deleted from chat instantly), and your position is live on-chain.

Security

Your keys. Your funds.
Always.

AES-256-GCM encryption

Private keys encrypted before touching the database.

PBKDF2 · 310,000 iterations

Per-user random salt. 12-byte IV. Brute-force resistant.

Short-lived signing

The key is decrypted only after the user provides a password for that action. The encrypted key remains at rest in SQLite.

Instant chat erasure

Password replies deleted from Telegram via deleteMessage immediately.

On-chain transparency

Your own PredictManager. Verify on any Sui explorer. Export your key anytime.

Pre-trade risk guard

Bot checks Predict registry and vault utilisation before every mint.

Rate limits

  • 10 open positions / user
  • 10 trades / hour
  • 3 requests / second / user
  • 3 copy leaders max
